Recipe: Apple Pie


Ingredients:

  • 4 apples
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 250g flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

    • Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. Peel the apples and cut them into thin slices.
    3. Mix the sugar and eggs in a large bowl.
    4. Add the melted butter and the flour with the baking powder.
    5. Add the apples to the mixture and pour everything into a baking pan.
    6. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown.

A Game of Thrones - Book Information

Game of Thrones

Author: George R. R. Martin

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Political Intrigue

Publication Year: 1996

Description:

"A Game of Thrones" is the first book in the epic fantasy series "A Song of Ice and Fire" by George R. R. Martin. The novel is set in the fictional world of Westeros, where noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne. The story is known for its complex characters, intricate plots, and unexpected twists. The book explores themes of power, betrayal, loyalty, and family, creating a rich and immersive world that has captivated readers worldwide. It introduces iconic characters such as Eddard Stark, Jon Snow, Daenerys Targaryen, and Tyrion Lannister.
